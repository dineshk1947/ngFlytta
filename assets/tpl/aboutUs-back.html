What is FLYTTA?

Flytta is a one stop relocation shop.

It is a solution for corporates and professionals, providing end to end service for their relocation.

Moving due to your job or a personal reason within your city or to another city, Flytta provides all your relocation needs from renting a house, to mobilise packers and movers, settling in services including assistance to schooling, DTH, telecom, gas, home maker and essentials, home services and, city experiences.
