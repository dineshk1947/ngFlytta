How it works ?


1. Discover


Explore your options on goflytta.com.Take advantage of our knowledge to discover which neighbourhood would make your perfect home. Our portal makes it more personalized based on your inputs to suit your lifestyle requirements. Learn more about the location and also discover other services which include Packers and Movers, Telecom, Internet, In House Appliances, Furniture and Other utilities like Gas, Govt. ID cards, etc., for free no-strings advice.


2. Wishlist


Add favourite services to your relocation package and review it anytime. You can always change before confirmation.


3. Choose


Once you have an idea of where to relocate i.e. particular area, you can choose up to 5 house visits before finalizing one.



4. Request to Book


Click the "Request to Book button" at the bottom of your cart, the package that you have chosen.


5. Confirmation


We will assign you a personal booking consultant who will contact you swiftly, to confirm details about your choice of Property, Moving assistance and other Services. They will also assist you with your queries. We will confirm the availability of your request and schedule the site visit. If you are an outstation traveller, we can also arrange the transportation as per your request.


6. Visit the Site


Our Executive will show you the site and share details of the properties you have chosen.


7. Request Additional Services


You may also request for additional services you may We will assist you with the information and booking.



8. Finalize your Relocation


We will send you a confirmation authorization email to you, within one to three days of confirmation, you will receive your contract. Your personal booking consultant will guide you thoroughly through the documentation process and verification.



9. Pay the amount


You can pay your rent and deposit (if there is one) through credit /debit card or bank transfer. We will help you through the process with the landlord and provide a copy of the signed contract.




10. Relocate Hassle Free


With all set to relocate, we welcome you with all the comforts and services at your door step and we are pleased to have worked with you. Kindly share your experience with us.
